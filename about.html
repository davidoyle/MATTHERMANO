About

I’m Matt Hermano, a Vancouver-based singer-songwriter writing late-night songs about missed calls, quiet streets, and the moments that stay with us longer than we expect.

You’ll usually find me playing in cafes and open mic nights around the city, where I test new songs in front of close listeners and shape each set one room at a time.

I care about intimate performances, honest lyrics, and creating a space where people can slow down and listen. If you want to bring that energy to your event, head to the booking page and reach out.

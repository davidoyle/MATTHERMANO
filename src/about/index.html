About

Matt Hermano is an R&B/soul songwriter blending warm melodies with stripped-back honesty.

His live sets are intimate and cinematic, built for rooms that reward attention and feeling.

“I write for that moment when a room goes quiet and everyone is suddenly in the same song.”
